About Us
History
Established in 2016 by the A.J. Mark Corporation, Grand Cedar Homes stands as a testament to visionary urban planning and community development. Nestled on 13.5 hectares of meticulously designed landscape, this subdivision boasts 1850 residential units, providing homes for a vibrant community of 9000 individuals. With approximately 1400 units actively engaged in its Homeowners Association, Grand Cedar Homes embodies a spirit of collective stewardship and community engagement. Continuously evolving and expanding, it remains a beacon of modern living, offering a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and community spirit for its residents.
